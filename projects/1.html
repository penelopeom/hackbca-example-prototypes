HOW TO MAKE A BOT CRY

Project Description: The differentiating factor between man and robot is the robots inability to feel. Some believe this to be an advantage, some a disadvantage. A holistic approach to understanding this fundamental idea is to recognize the true benefit to an emotion filled robot: simulating its misery. In this course, each individual will simulate a robot's sadness by destroying every last thing it has been programmed to hold near and dear to understand and demonstrate the benefit of emotion-filled robots: fulfilling sadistic rage.
